About This App

I plan to design this app to help it's users understand their financial situation regarding their active or potential loans.

In order to achieve this, I will be giving them the means to:
- Calculate their payments in advance
- To be able to compare their exisitng/planned loans,
- To provide a suggestion on how to most effectively repay those loans.

Disclaimer:

No personal data is collected or stored. All information provided is solely for calculation purposes and remains confidential during the session.
